Project Description

BMW Car Price Analysis & Prediction

This project focuses on uncovering patterns and relationships in BMW’s global car sales data from 2010 to 2024. The primary objective is to analyze how different factors such as model, region, color, engine size, and transmission type influence both sales performance and pricing behavior.

The dataset provides a unique opportunity to explore market dynamics, evaluate product performance across regions, and develop a simple predictive model for price estimation.
Through detailed visualization and statistical analysis, this project aims to generate actionable business insights for automotive market strategy.

Goals

  1. Explore BMW sales trends and model popularity across global regions.
  2. Identify how car features (e.g., engine size, color, transmission, fuel type) affect price and sales volume.
  3. Visualize correlations between market regions, customer preferences, and price distribution.
  4. Predict car price using regression techniques as an optional modeling component.

Proposed Dataset:

Source: Kaggle - BMW Worldwide Sales Records (2010–2024)

The dataset contains BMW’s sales and specifications data across multiple regions worldwide. It includes over 30,000 entries and features such as car model, engine size, year, region, transmission type, fuel type, mileage, color, price, and sales volume.

region_model_sales = data.groupby(["Region", "Model"])["Sales_Volume"].sum().reset_index()
pivot_region_model = region_model_sales.pivot(index="Region", columns="Model", values="Sales_Volume").fillna(0)
pivot_region_model.plot(kind="bar", stacked=True, figsize=(10,6), colormap="coolwarm")
